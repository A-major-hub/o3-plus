MENTAL HEALTH AWARENESS COMPAIGN.

Mental health is a basic human right for all, Everyone, whoever, and wherever they are , has the right to the highest attainable standard of mental health.

Good mental health is vital to all our overall health well-being.

RATING IN UGANDA

24.4% of adults and 22.9% of children are affected by mental health conditions in Uganda with 33% being adults experiencing severe Anxiety and 18% with moderate depression. anxiety and depression are now the leading mental health disorders in Uganda.

ON RECORD

616,000 mental health outpatient cases were recorded in 2023-2024 first year with 78% under the age of 30 years .
